🌾 Our Story

At HistoricRoots, we believe the future of food lies in the wisdom of the past. For generations, India’s native rice varieties nourished communities with their natural strength, diversity, and resilience. Over time, many of these ancient grains disappeared from everyday kitchens — replaced by polished, uniform rice.

We set out to bring them back. By working closely with traditional farmers and heritage grain cultivators, we revive rare native rice varieties and transform them into simple, nourishing foods for modern lifestyles.

🌱 Why Native Rice?

  • Naturally unpolished
  • Rich in whole grain nutrition
  • Grown using time-tested farming wisdom
  • Deeply rooted in regional food culture

Each bowl reconnects you with grains that once sustained generations.

❓ Detailed FAQ

What makes this mix different?

Made from traditional native rice varieties with natural fiber and nutrients.

Is it suitable for daily consumption?

Yes, it can be consumed daily as a healthy breakfast or snack option.

How to cook?

Mix 2 tbsp of health mix with 1 cup water or milk. Cook for 3–5 minutes while stirring continuously until smooth.

Can babies consume it?

Yes, it can be given as a thin porridge after gradual introduction. Please consult a pediatrician if unsure.

Is it good for fitness and weight-conscious diets?

Yes, it supports fitness and weight-conscious diets when consumed as part of a balanced lifestyle.

Is it suitable for balanced sugar diets?

It can be a better alternative when consumed moderately as part of a balanced diet.

Is it gluten-free?

Naturally gluten-free. However, check for cross-contamination if you have strict gluten sensitivity.

What else can it be used for?

Can be used for dosa, pancakes, malt, smoothies, and healthy snacks.

How should it be stored?

Store in an airtight container in a cool, dry place.

What is the shelf life?

Best before 3 months due to no preservatives.
